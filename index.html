ARRI ALEXA SXT PLUS

The most versatile of the ALEXA SXT digital cinema cameras, the ARRI ALEXA SXT Plus Camera Body (LDS PL) features built-in, wireless camera and lens control, tilt and roll sensors, a fourth monitor-out port, and a third RS/accessory connector. SXT Plus saves time on set by enabling you to plug lens motors directly into the camera, no motor control box is needed.

ARRI ALEXA SXT W

The ALEXA SXT W Camera Body from ARRI (LDS PL) expands upon the design of the popular ALEXA SXT Plus with the addition of an integrated wireless video transmitter. Transmitter features include a range up to 2000', under 1 ms delay, uncompressed video signals, and 128-bit AES encryption. Plug your lens motors directly into the camera; no motor control box is needed.

ARRI ALEXA MINI

With its compact, lightweight carbon body, the ALEXA Mini Body with 4:3 and ARRIRAW complements the range of full-size ALEXA cameras, and is especially useful for capturing specialized shots. The self-contained ALEXA Mini can record UHD-resolution images with up to 14 stops of dynamic range. Its 35mm-sized CMOS sensor features a titanium mount and a 4:3 aspect ratio.

ARRI ALEXA XT

Keeping with the form factor of Arri’s original Alexa camera while incorporating more recent feature advancements, the Alexa XT can be fitted with a Panavision lens mount and further supported with the Panavision Modular accessory system for streamlined on-set functionality.

ARRI ALEXA LF BIG

Same features as ALEXA SXT W with Large Format Sensor and 14+ stops for creative exploration. 3x sensor modes: LF Open Gate, LF 16:9, LF 2.39:1. Capture ARRIRAW or Apple ProRes to SXR Capture Drives or SxS PRO+ Cards. Includes LPL Mount & lens adapter.

MASTER PRIME

The Master Prime series includes lenses with focal lengths of 16mm, 25mm, 35mm, 50mm, 75mm, and 100mm. These lenses are known for their exceptional optical performance, providing ultra-sharp images, minimal distortion, and high contrast. They are widely used in professional filmmaking.

Ultra Prime lenses are available in focal lengths of 16mm, 24mm, 35mm, 50mm, 85mm, and 135mm. These lenses from Carl Zeiss offer high optical performance with minimal distortion and excellent color rendition, making them popular choices for cinematographers seeking high-quality images.

SIGMA

Sigma lenses are available in focal lengths of 14mm, 24mm, 35mm, 50mm, 85mm, 105mm, and 135mm. Known for their sharpness and versatility, Sigma lenses are valued by filmmakers for their optical quality and affordability compared to other high-end cinema lenses.

ALURA ZOOM

The Alura Zoom series includes the 45-250mm zoom lens. These zoom lenses offer a versatile range of focal lengths, allowing cinematographers to capture a variety of shots without changing lenses. They are known for their consistent image quality throughout the zoom range.

ACCESSORIES

RONIN 2 GIMBAL

The Ronin 2 is a professional-grade camera stabilizer designed for cinematographers seeking smooth and stable footage. It provides three-axis stabilization for a wide range of camera setups, allowing filmmakers to capture steady shots while walking, running, or during dynamic movements.

MOVIE PRO

Movie Pro is a versatile camera control system that offers remote control over various camera settings and functions. It enables filmmakers to adjust parameters such as focus, zoom, aperture, and even camera movement remotely, providing greater flexibility and precision in capturing shots.

STEADICAM

Steadicam is a camera stabilization system that uses a mechanical arm and harness to isolate the camera operator's movements from the camera itself. It allows for smooth, steady shots even when the operator is walking or moving quickly, providing a floating effect that enhances the cinematic quality of the footage.

C MOTION

C Motion is a wireless follow focus system used in filmmaking to remotely control the focus of a camera lens. It allows camera operators or focus pullers to adjust focus wirelessly, providing precise control over focus points during filming without physically touching the camera setup.

NUCLEUS

Nucleus is a wireless lens control system designed for remote control of focus, iris, and zoom on cinema lenses. It offers filmmakers the ability to control lens parameters wirelessly, providing flexibility and precision in capturing shots, especially in situations where manual adjustment of lens settings is challenging.

SHOGUN MONITOR

The Shogun Monitor is a high-resolution external monitor designed for use with professional cinema cameras. It offers a large, high-quality display for monitoring footage in real-time, providing accurate color representation and exposure evaluation during filming. It also often includes advanced features like waveform monitoring and focus peaking to assist filmmakers in achieving optimal results.
